# Supermarket Kit — Production Specification

## Exterior
Fictional bilingual sign zone, glass entrance, trolley bay, delivery entrance, loading dock, parking modules, roof AC units, utility meters and shaded display zone.

## Interior modules
Checkout counter, scanner, conveyor, baskets, trolleys, 1 m and 2 m shelving bays, drinks cooler, freezer, produce rack, bakery counter, cosmetics bay, storage pallet, cartons, office desk, ceiling-light strip and camera prop.

## Scale
- Aisle clear width: 1.5–2.0 m.
- Shelf module: 1.0 m wide × 0.5 m deep × 2.0 m high.
- Checkout lane: 0.9 m wide × 3.0 m long.
- Door clear width: 1.8 m double sliding.

## Budgets
Exterior shell 5,000–12,000 triangles; enterable interior shell 8,000–18,000; each shelf 300–1,200; checkout 600–2,000. Shared 1024 atlas, maximum four materials per room cluster. Use box collisions and interaction markers rather than per-product collision.
