# Café and Restaurant Kit — Production Specification

## Archetypes
Arabic café, karak shop, Indian restaurant, bakery, shawarma shop, seafood restaurant, waterfront café and casual street café. All branding is fictional.

## Modular components
Three storefront widths, sign band, glazed door, service counter, menu-board socket, kitchen hatch, outdoor terrace tile, table, chair, umbrella, queue rail, takeaway shelf, display case and delivery pickup marker.

## Budgets
Storefront 1,500–5,000 triangles; interior room 4,000–12,000; table/chair set 250–1,200; counter 500–2,000. Maximum three materials per storefront, 1024 atlas, 512 prop atlas. Box collisions; chairs use one simplified seat/back collision only when interactive.

## Readability
Entrances, counters and interaction zones must remain recognizable at 5 m and 15 m on a six-inch display. Sign silhouettes and awnings carry archetype identity; text is secondary.
